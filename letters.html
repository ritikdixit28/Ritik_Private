Letters from your BUBU

Open when you miss me, okay?

Letter 1 — Morning

Dear Lado, I love the sleepy way you say hello. I love that your first thought is me. I love that your day gets lighter when I remind you to drink water and smile. I love that you’re my reason for trying harder and softer at the same time.

When the world feels loud, come here. I’ll hold your hand through the noise. — Your BUBU

Letter 2 — After a long day

Some days are heavy, and that’s okay. Sit with me. Tell me everything. We’ll let the tough parts be small together. I’m not going anywhere — not now, not ever.

We’re a team. With you, I’m home.

Letter 3 — Future

I can’t wait for our grocery runs, our Sunday naps, our little traditions. I want your laughter in our kitchen and your name on my favorite mug.

Thank you for choosing us, again and again.

Letter 4 — Just because

You’re lovely as you are. You don’t have to earn rest or love. You are worthy of softness and peace — and I’ll spend my life proving it.
